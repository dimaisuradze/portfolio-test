DIMITRI MAISURADZE

BRUSSELS, BE / ART DIRECTION / CREATIVE ENGINEERING / SCENOGRAPHY

I am a Brussels-based Art Director and Creative Engineer. I specialize in bridging the gap between high-level aesthetic vision and the gritty, logistical reality of getting things built. Whether it’s a film set, a bespoke brand pop-up, or a large-scale institutional art installation, I turn concepts into physical space.

Most creative studios face a persistent problem: highly creative people are often a nightmare with budgets and logistics, while traditional project managers lack visual taste. I operate entirely in that overlap. I understand the physics of hands-on fabrication, but I am equally comfortable writing a €21K technical grant dossier, sourcing specific recycled materials, and managing the supplier spreadsheets.

For Art Directors, Producers, and Creative Agencies, I act as heavy artillery. I manage the floor, engineer the structural problem-solving—like figuring out how to safely hang a 6.5m mosaic with only 50 anchor points—and control the tempo so the core design team can stay glued to the monitor without panicking about the physics.

From fast-paced commercial campaigns (YSL, Nike) and international feature films, to orchestrating complex, one-of-a-kind brand experiences, my goal is to eliminate friction. I ensure the creative vision is executed flawlessly, on schedule, and strictly on brief.
