DIMITRI MAISURADZE

BRUSSELS, BE / ASST. ART DIRECTOR / SET BUILDER

I am a Brussels-based Assistant Art Director and physical set builder. My primary job is to bridge the gap between a Production Designer’s vision and the logistical reality of a shooting set.

Coming from a background of hands-on fabrication and woodworking, I understand the actual physics of how sets are built, not just how they look on a moodboard. For Art Directors, I act as a protective layer. I manage the floor, handle supplier logistics, and control the crew tempo so the core design team can stay glued to the monitor and focus purely on the aesthetic.

Whether it's live-sketching a last-minute set change in 60 seconds to avoid waiting for 3D renders, or pulling out the tools to engineer an on-the-fly fix while the camera is rolling, my goal is to eliminate friction.

From fast-paced commercial campaigns (YSL, Carrefour, AXE) to international feature films (Lords of War, Vigilante), I ensure the art department delivers flawlessly, on schedule, and strictly on brief.
